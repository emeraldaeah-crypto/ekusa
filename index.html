baa

baa

oil on canvas,private collection
oil on canvas,private collection

Alongside the painting, only the sound baa first surfaced; in time it revealed itself as Baal. In the Old Testament, Baal appears as a god associated with idolatry, later merging and changing through Baal-zebub, or Beelzebub, the Lord of the Flies, and demonological imagination. In Semitic languages the word means lord, and belongs to the ancient worlds of Canaan, Phoenicia, and Syria. As a divine or spirit name, Baal attaches to storm, sun, fertility, and other forces of nature. Here, a ghost, peekaboo, six layers, a green heptagram, leeches, flame, three triangles, blue and red, pink and green gather into a bit-like, quantized baa. Gold becomes soil that carries what it will bear.

絵と共にbaaというおとだけが浮かんできた、いつしかそれがbaalだときづく。バー・baalについて旧約聖書では「偶像崇拝」の神、後世、バアル・ゼブブ蠅の王悪魔とも習合・変化したもの。セム語で「主（しゅ）」古代カナン、フェニキア、シリアの存在。神名・精霊名として、嵐・太陽・豊穣などの自然神の「バール」が冠される。おばけ、いないないバー・6層階層、緑七芒星、ヒル、炎。三角が3つ。青と赤。ピンクと緑。bit、量子化されているbaa。黄金は孕む土。
